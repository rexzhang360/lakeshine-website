Service Process · 服務流程

House & Townhouse Cleaning

獨立屋/聯排屋清潔服務 — A systematic, top-to-bottom clean. Same dedicated cleaner every visit. 每次同一位清潔員。

⏱ 2.5–4.5 hours 💰 From $210 +HST ✅ 100% Satisfaction 🛡 $2M Insured
📅
Step 01
Booking Confirmation
預約確認
Book online at lakeshinecleaning.ca or via WeChat. You receive a confirmation with your cleaner's name, arrival window, and a full checklist of what will be cleaned. 網上預約後收到確認，包含清潔員姓名、到達時間及清潔清單。
24–48 hrs before visit
🚪
Step 02
Arrival & Setup
到達及準備
Cleaner arrives within the confirmed window, puts on shoe covers, and does a quick walkthrough to note any special requests or areas of focus. 清潔員按時到達，穿鞋套，快速確認特別需求及重點清潔區域。
0–10 min
🍳
Step 03
Kitchen Deep Clean
廚房清潔
Countertops, stovetop (degreaser applied), sink, appliance exteriors, cabinet fronts, backsplash, microwave exterior. 台面、爐台（去油劑）、水槽、電器外部、廚櫃正面、後擋板、微波爐外部。
20–30 min
🚿
Step 04
Bathroom(s) Sanitize
浴室消毒清潔
Toilet inside and out, sink, tub or shower, mirrors, all fixtures, floors. CLR applied for hard water deposits on faucets and showerheads. Full disinfection spray on all surfaces. 馬桶內外、洗手盆、浴缸/淋浴間、鏡子、水龍頭、地板，CLR除水垢，全面消毒噴霧。
15–25 min per bathroom
🛏
Step 05
Living Areas & Bedrooms
客廳及睡房
Dusting all surfaces top-to-bottom (including baseboards and window sills), vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors with the right product for your floor type (Bona for hardwood), wiping light switches and door handles. 由高到低撣灰（含踢腳板及窗台），吸塵，按地板類型拖地，清潔開關及門把。
20–40 min
✅
Step 06
Final Check & Feedback
完工確認及反饋
A final walkthrough (with you if present) or photo documentation sent to you. We message you within 3 hours to ask for feedback. Not satisfied with any area? We return within 24 hours to re-clean at no charge. 完工確認，3小時內詢問反饋。任何地方不滿意，24小時內免費返工。
